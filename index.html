Open source activity summary

A group-level commitment, not a single project.

TD uses its public GitHub presence, alongside memberships in industry foundations, to help build financial technology that is more simple, more helpful, and more human — in the open, and together with the wider community.

Foundational membership

FINOS and the Linux Foundation

TD joined the Fintech Open Source Foundation as a Platinum Member, with a seat on the FINOS Board, alongside membership in the Linux Foundation.

Patent protection

Open Invention Network 2.0

TD committed to the upgraded OIN 2.0 Program in 2026, extending open source patent protection to new technologies, including AI.

Community leadership

Open Source in Finance Forum, Toronto

TD was a founding sponsor of OSFF Toronto, with a keynote on the role of open source and financial institutions in the foundation of AI.

Standards contribution

Post-quantum cryptography migration

TD contributed to FS-ISAC's published timeline for post-quantum cryptographic migration across the financial sector.
